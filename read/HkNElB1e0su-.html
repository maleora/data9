Best Friends: The Diaries of Worm and Spider

These are the diaries... of a worm and a spider. Surprisingly, they aren't that different from you or me. Except Worm eats his homework and Spider enjoys taking wind-catching lessons. Doreen Cronin, the New York Times best-selling author of Click, Clack, Moo and Giggle, Giggle, Quack teams up with Harry Bliss for these hysterical diaries about the daily doings and the hidden worlds of these lovable bugs.
