Selected Poems of Salvador Espriu

This selection, translated from the catalan, marks the debut of Salvador Espriu in this country. One of Spain's great twentieth-century poets, who spent his life in the pursuit of 'the hard prize of a few bare words,' Espriu viewed his nine major books of poems (approximately eight poems from each are represented here) as a single meditation on death. In his spare, rarely rhymed verse, the simplest words are burnished...
