The Claudius Novels

I, Claudius (1934) is a novel by English writer Robert Graves, written in the form of an autobiography of the Roman Emperor Claudius. Accordingly, it includes history of the Julio-Claudian Dynasty & the Roman Empire, from Julius Caesar's assassination in 44 BC to Caligula's assassination in 41 AD.
