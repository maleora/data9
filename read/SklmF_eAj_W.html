Kothi Kommachi is the first of two autobiographic volumes of Dr. Mullapudi Venkataramana (June 1931- February 2011), writer, film-maker, and a legend in Telugu literature. Mullapudi Venkata Ramana is a Telugu story writer, known for his hilarious style of writing.
