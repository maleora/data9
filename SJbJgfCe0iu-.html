More About I Got You, Babe

This was my first book by Jane Graves.I just did not want to put it down, it just kept me wondering what was going to happened next. Loved the strong willed Renee. Enjoyed the storyline and the two characters! Cannot wait to see her other books! Good story, went on without really feeling like it moved all the much forward a little too long. Kept picturing hero as an older paunchy guy. Had to go remind myself of characters had in mind by looking at the cover. Liked how it was all resolved. That made up for any other shortcomings in the book. Cute story 3 1/2 stars.
