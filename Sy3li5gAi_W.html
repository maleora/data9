More About The Revolutionist

Wow. Great book! Well researched and compelling. A fun way to learn about the communist revolution Serviceable but ultimately mediocre thriller. At least it doesn't paint the Revolution as wholly negative and Felix Yusupov vs. his sexy Bolshevik twin sister Lili gives the book a bit of novelty value. An excellent story that brings to life the idealism behind the Russian Revolution and the grim reality of the Stalinist world. Readable and enjoyable, but dense enough to paint the characters well. Definitely recommend.
