I really enjoyed the first book and was looking forward to reading the second one. Archie is a great character and I wanted to disappear into his world of geekiness again, to see what he would come up with next, what strange event would he have to face. He is feeling awkward after confessing his love for Sarah and being turned down,... Archie is one of my favourite male characters in YA fiction. He (and his inner monologue IM) is so funny, observant, realistic and geeky!
